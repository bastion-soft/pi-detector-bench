# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/), version 2.1.

In short: be respectful, assume good faith, and keep criticism technical. Rigorous,
adversarial scrutiny of the methodology and results is **welcome and encouraged** —
attacks on people are not.

Report unacceptable behaviour by opening a confidential issue or emailing the
maintainers at **conduct@bastionsoft.com**. Maintainers will review and respond, and
will respect the privacy and security of the reporter.
